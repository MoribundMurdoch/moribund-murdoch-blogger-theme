The Moribund Institute

The Moribund Institute is a one-man archival and educational operation run by MoribundMurdoch — a philologer, lexicographer, and accelerator of ideas. The name is deliberately bleak. Moribund means on the verge of dying. The Institute exists to rescue things from oblivion — words, ideas, histories, and whatever else deserves a longer life than the internet usually grants.

The word dies on the tongue if no one writes it down. — The Institute's working principle

Content here spans the full Dewey Decimal range: language and linguistics, philosophy and economics, history, technology, K-drama, cast iron, and raw milk. The only thread is that everything is worth knowing more precisely than most people bother to.
